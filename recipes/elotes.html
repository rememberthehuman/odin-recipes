Elotes

Mexican corn on the cob!

One of the most popular street foods in mexicos, you'll find vendors on the street selling corn similiar to this!

Ingredients

  • 4 ears corn, shucked
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup grated cotija cheese
  • Chopped cilantro
  • 4 lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
  2. Grill corn until hot and lightly charred all over, 7 to 10 minutes, depending on the temperature of the grill. Roll the ears in melted butter, then spread evenly with mayonnaise. Sprinkle with cotija cheese, cilantro and serve with a lime wedge.
